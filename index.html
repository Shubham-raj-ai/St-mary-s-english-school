Transport

The need for safe passage of each child to school and back home is of paramount important to us. To ensure safe travel the school has its own fleet of out sourced school buses designed as per standards and manned by trained drivers and personnel sensitized to the needs of small children. The Transport Service (optional) is operated by the school and a separate transport fee is charged for this.
